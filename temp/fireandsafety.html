We are one of the private organizations in Fire Protection Systems

We offers our services because every year, fire causes deaths and damages to property & costly materials. In that scenario we deal in all kinds of fire fighting and fire preventive systems as well as life safety equipment.

We are specilised in the Supply, Installation, Testing, Commissioning, Servicing and Maintenance of various Fire Protection and Alarm Systems. Our highly qualified and well-experienced team of Engineers is able to Install and Commission the system to meet the requirement of NFPA and other International & Local Authorities Requirements.

Range of Systems We offer

  • All Types of Fire Extinguishers:(i.e) CO2, Dry Chemical Powder, Foam, Water, Wet Chemical and Helotron
  • Dry and Wet Standpipe Systems,
  • Fire Hydrants (all types)
  • Sprinkler System
  • Deluge System
  • Water Mist System
  • FM 200 Suppression System
  • Foam System
  • Carbon Dioxide System
  • Gas Detection System
  • Wet Chemical Suppression System
  • Fire Alarm System (Addressable and Conventional)
  • Industrial Safety
  • Monitors
  • Pumps
  • Emergency Lighting System and more.

We also deal with Installation, Maintanance, and Commissioning for the systems above we supply.
